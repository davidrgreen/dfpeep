Recommendations:

DFPeep suggests the following issues be examined.

1 Error:

  • Disabled Initial Load Too Late

    googletag.pubads().disableInitialLoad() likely had no effect because it was called after googletag.enableServices().

1 Warning:

  • Moved Slot Element After Rendered In DOM

    When an ad slot element is moved in the DOM after the initial fetching of the ad it will force the ad's iframe to refresh, resulting in a blank slot. The following slots have been detected as having been moved in the DOM after the initial fetching of their ads:

    • div-id-for-bottom-300x250
    • div-id-for-bottom-728x90
    • div-id-for-middle-300x250
    • div-id-for-middle-970x250
    • div-id-for-middle2-970x250
    • div-id-for-promobar-980x30
    • div-id-for-skin
    • div-id-for-top-300x250
    • div-id-for-top-728x90

    If you need to move the slot element, such as moving a sidebar ad inline on mobile, then you need to ensure the slot element is moved before the ad is fetched. A sure-fire way of doing this is to use googletag.pubads().disableInitialLoad(), allowing you to manually fetch the ad with googletag.pubads().refresh() only after the slot element has been moved in the DOM.
